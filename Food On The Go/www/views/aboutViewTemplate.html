This app is undoubdetly an instant foodie-favorite! Running late and hungry? We at FoodOnTheGo! are here to help :) We provide you this amazing facility to place a dine-in order at your favorite nearby restaurants even before you reach there! It uses your current location, suggests cool places to eat, allows you to select a restaurant and place your order. Dive-in to your favorite delicacies as soon as you reach there! It can also help you place an order at a nearby restaurant for home delivery. You can search for restaurants too, in case you want to order for friends!
